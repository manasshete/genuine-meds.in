Frequently Asked Questions

Can you provide medical advice?

No, we do not provide medical advice. GenuineMeds.in is an educational platform that provides drug information. Always consult with a healthcare professional for medical advice.

How often is your drug information updated?

We regularly review and update our drug database to ensure accuracy. Updates are made based on the latest medical guidelines and regulatory information.

Can I suggest a drug to be added to your database?

Yes! We welcome suggestions for new drugs to include in our database. Please use the contact form above with the subject "Drug Information Question" to make your suggestion.

I found incorrect information. How can I report it?

We take accuracy seriously. Please use the contact form with the subject "Information Correction" and provide details about the incorrect information you found.
